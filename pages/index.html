Vision

JAGRITI, our flagship cultural event, formerly known as Cera-Fest, has been permanently established as a vibrant celebration aimed at fostering deep connections and strengthening the bond among people from diverse regions across the country. It serves as a unifying platform where cultures blend, friendships flourish, and the spirit of togetherness thrives.

Learn More

Aim

Jagriti's main aim is to break free from the monotonous routine of everyday life, offering a refreshing escape where one can truly unwind, rejuvenate, and feel alive. It is a chance to embrace joy, relaxation, and renewal. Let us come together to celebrate this event with boundless energy, enthusiasm, and excitement!
