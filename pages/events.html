Surakriti
Surakriti invites collegiate singing enthusiasts to showcase their talent in an electrifying solo singing competition. Open to all college students, this event is a perfect platform to mesmerize the audience with your voice and creativity. Participants are given a total time of 6 minutes, including a 1-minute sound check. Any performance exceeding the limit will result in a deduction of marks, so timing is crucial. The stage will be equipped with adequate microphones and plug-ins, but participants are required to bring their own instruments. This is more than just a contest—it’s an opportunity to express your musical flair and connect with fellow music lovers. Rules and regulations may be updated at the discretion of the organizers to ensure a seamless event. Join us for Surakriti and let your voice resonate on a grand stage. Your journey to becoming the next solo sensation starts here. Don’t miss this opportunity to shine!

Anukriti
Anukriti: Inter-College Dance Competition is the ultimate stage for college dancers to showcase their talent and creativity. Open to all collegiate groups, this competition embraces diverse dance forms, allowing participants to perform in Solo, Duet, and Group categories with styles ranging from Eastern, Western, or a Fusion of both.
