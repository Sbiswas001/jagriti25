Surakriti

Solo Singing Competition

Step onto the grand stage of Surakriti, the ultimate solo singing competition for college students! Designed for singing enthusiasts, this event is your moment to captivate an audience with your talent, passion, and unique musical expressions. The stage will be equipped with high-quality microphones and plug-ins, ensuring optimal sound quality for every performance. However, participants must bring their own instruments to suit their style.

Take this chance to showcase your talent, connect with fellow artists, and compete for glory. Join Surakriti and let the music within you shine!
