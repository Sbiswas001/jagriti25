Musicon

Battle of Bands

Musicon: Battle of Bands is an exciting inter-college event that invites talented collegiate bands to compete and showcase their musical prowess. This is your chance to take the stage, perform live, and make your mark in the world of music! Each band will get 14 minutes, including 10 minutes for the performance and 4 minutes for the sound check. Exceeding this time limit will lead to penalties, so ensure you plan your set carefully. The organizers will provide essential equipment such as drums, microphones, plug-ins, and sound arrangements, but bands must bring their own instruments. Judges will evaluate based on creativity, technical skills, and stage presence, so bring your best performance forward. Rules and regulations may be subject to changes as decided by the organizers, so stay informed.
