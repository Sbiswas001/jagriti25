Anukriti

Dance Competition

Anukriti: Inter-College Dance Competition is the ultimate stage for college dancers to showcase their talent and creativity. Participants are responsible for arranging their costumes, makeup, props, and stage setup to enhance their performances. The competition promises a vibrant and energetic atmosphere, celebrating the passion and dedication of dancers. Rhythmania is your chance to set the stage on fire, captivate the audience, and prove your mettle. So, gather your team, fine-tune your moves, and get ready to own the stage!

Timeline

The event registration is ongoing. The event will take place in GCECT campus.

Eligibility

The event is open to all, with no restrictions as such.

Rules

Solo: 4+1 minutes, Duet: 5+1 minutes (1 minute for stage setup) Group (3-10 members): 6+2 minutes (2 minutes for stage setup)
