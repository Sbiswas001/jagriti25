AAKRITI

Fashion Show

Welcome to AAKRITI, an inter-college fashion show competition that brings creativity and style to the stage! Registration is open to any college group, with a maximum of 16 participants per team. Each team gets a time limit of 10+2 minutes, including sound check, and exceeding this limit will result in mark deductions.

The registration fee is ₹1199 per team, while on-spot registration costs ₹1399 per team. Get ready to showcase your fashion and flair in this exciting competition!
