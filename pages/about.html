About GCECT and Jagriti

Government College of Engineering and Ceramic Technology (GCECT), formerly known as College of Ceramic Technology (CCT), is a premier government-funded autonomous engineering institute in Kolkata. With a stellar academic reputation and alumni network, GCECT combines excellence with cultural and social development through events like JAGRITI.

JAGRITI celebrates talent, innovation, and creativity with a diverse range of performances, competitions, and social initiatives.
